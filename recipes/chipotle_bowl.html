Description

This homemade Chipotle Bowl is a hearty and customizable meal inspired by the popular fast-casual favorite. It features a vibrant mix of seasoned rice, grilled chicken or steak, black beans, corn salsa, guacamole, and a drizzle of spicy chipotle sauce.

Perfect for meal prep or a family dinner, this bowl delivers bold Mexican-inspired flavors in every bite. You can easily adapt it to suit different dietary needsâ€”swap the protein, use cauliflower rice, or go heavy on the veggies for a healthy twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 lb grilled chicken or steak, sliced
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn salsa (or corn, diced tomatoes, and onions)
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chipotle sauce (or your favorite dressing)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  2. Grill the chicken or steak until fully cooked, then slice into strips.
  3. In a bowl, layer the cooked rice, followed by black beans, corn salsa, and sliced protein.
  4. Add a dollop of guacamole and sprinkle with shredded cheese if desired.
  5. Drizzle with chipotle sauce and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve immediately or store in the fridge for meal prep.
