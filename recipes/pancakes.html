Description

These fluffy homemade pancakes are perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch. They’re light, tender, and delicious when topped with butter, syrup, fruit, or even a dollop of whipped cream.

Quick to make with simple ingredients, this pancake recipe is ideal for both beginners and experienced cooks. You can easily customize them by adding blueberries, chocolate chips, or cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined (don’t overmix).
  4. Heat a lightly greased skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
